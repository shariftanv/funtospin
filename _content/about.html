About FunToSpin

FunToSpin is a free, interactive online random wheel spinner tool designed for making decisions, adding fun to games, and solving debates — all with a spin!

Whether you're a teacher picking a student, a party host deciding on dares, or a friend group trying to choose what to eat, our colorful and customizable spin wheel makes decision-making simple and exciting.

Popular Uses

  • Classroom activities (student picker)
  • Raffle draws and prize giveaways
  • Random name selection
  • Truth or dare games
  • Meal or activity choosers
  • Daily decision maker

Key Features

  • Easy to customize with your own text
  • Choose from popular presets like fruits, colors, or party ideas
  • Confetti celebration on winning spin
  • No login or sign-up required
  • Mobile-friendly design

Our goal is to provide a simple and fun spinner wheel that works on any device and brings joy to everyday choices. Keep FunToSpin open during class, meetings, parties, or any decision-making moment!
